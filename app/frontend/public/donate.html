Why we accept donations

Even though we are a for-profit company, our work sits at the intersection of special education, autism support, communication access, and family empowerment. These are areas where the need is enormous, but funding is often limited.

Donations help us

  • Keep our AAC tool free forever
  • Offer discounted or free access to families in need
  • Partner with schools that lack funding
  • Build new features faster
  • Conduct real-world research with parents, teachers, and therapists

Where your contribution goes

  • Engineering and development
  • Accessibility improvements
  • AI research for communication support
  • Community partnerships
  • Training materials for families and specialists

A message from our founder

“Talkeez was born from my journey as a parent. I know what it feels like to search for tools that don't exist — and to wish someone would build something that truly helps. Your support helps us build those tools for families like mine.”

Ways to contribute

  • Monthly support
