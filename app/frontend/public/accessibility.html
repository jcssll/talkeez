Commitment to Accessibility

Talkeez is committed to making our tools accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities, non-verbal communicators, and diverse learning needs.

Standards We Follow

We aim to meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines, including:

  • Keyboard navigation
  • High-contrast modes
  • Screen-reader compatibility
  • Clear visual hierarchy
  • Simple, predictable layouts
  • Accessible color palettes
  • Alt text for images

Continuous Improvement

We regularly test our tools with:

  • Assistive technology users
  • Special education professionals
  • Parents and caregivers

Feedback directly informs our updates.
