How to use the Free AAC

Go to /aac.html in any modern browser. No login required. Tap the Fullscreen button for the best experience on a tablet.
Talkeez AAC uses your device's built-in voice (Web Speech API). When you tap a tile, that word is spoken. Tap multiple tiles to build a sentence, then press Speak.
Use the backspace button to remove the last word, the Clear button to start over, or tap any token in the sentence strip to remove it.
AAC works as long as your browser has cached the page and your device's voice engine is available. We recommend testing offline on the device your child will use.
