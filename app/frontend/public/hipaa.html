HIPAA Compliance Statement

Draft for review

Overview

Talkeez is not a HIPAA-covered entity. However, because we support communication, therapy, and special-education environments, we voluntarily follow HIPAA-aligned best practices to protect sensitive information.

How We Align With HIPAA Standards

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Secure authentication
  • Audit logging
  • Limited data access for staff
  • Vendor security reviews
  • No selling or sharing of personal information

Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)

If a district, clinic, or therapy provider requires a BAA, Talkeez can enter into one upon request.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not provide medical diagnoses
  • We do not replace clinical judgment
  • We do not use data to train public AI models
